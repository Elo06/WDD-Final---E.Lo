History

Since civilizations of antiquity, food has played a paramount role in our lives. Techniques, ingredients, and recipes continue
to evolve. Several great civilizations started producing grains, meat, salt, sugar, and other goods. Through trade, people ensured the flow
of culinary traditions, knowledge, and products. Nations embraced and implemented these ideas. As a result, today, we have a variety
of different cuisines and recipes.

Fun Facts

  • French: In Paris, baguettes are free!
  • Chinese: Chinese dishes always balanced these five flavors: salty, sweet, spicy, sour and bitter.
  • Greek: In Greek culinary culture, lunch in considered the biggest meal of the day.
  • Korean: Strong belief in yin and yang is always reflected in Korean courses.
  • Caribbean: Papaya is featured in most dishes.
